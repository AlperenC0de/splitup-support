Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a new group?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, enter your group name and description, then invite members via their email or username.

Can I use SplitUp offline?

Yes! SplitUp works offline and syncs your data when you're back online.

How do I settle a debt?

Go to the group, tap on the person you want to settle with, and select "Settle Up" to record the payment.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data and never share your information with third parties.
